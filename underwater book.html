🌊 Ocean & Life Underwater Book 🌊

Welcome to the Ocean 🌊

The ocean covers more than 70% of Earth and is home to many animals like fish, whales, dolphins, sharks, and turtles.

Kelp Forests 🌿

Kelp forests are underwater jungles made of giant seaweed that give food and shelter to many sea animals.

The Open Ocean 🌊

The open ocean is the largest habitat on Earth where whales, sharks, dolphins, and tuna travel long distances.

Ocean Life Connections 🐠

All ocean life is connected through food chains where every animal depends on another for survival.
